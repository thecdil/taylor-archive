Bibliometrics

Bibliometrics are the use of statistical methods to analyze books, articles and other publications, especially in scientific contents.

The following visualizations were created from authors who contributed to our Taylor Archive Digital Collection, dating back to 1970. Author names were standardized and searched in Web of Science, where their scholarly contributions were compiled.

The data was then visualized in VOSViewer to understand the scholarly impact of work that has been influenced by and the product of the Taylor Wilderness Research Station according to future citations of that work, co-authorship of papers, and fields of interest over time.
